Peep show? Faith Evans accidentally flashed the audience while performing at a Bad Boy Family Reunion concert in Boston on Saturday, September 24 — and Twitter users, naturally, went nuts.

During the show, the R&B singer, 43, was performing her hit “You Used to Love Me” at TD Garden arena when she slightly lifted up her long patterned white dress to avoid tripping over it. While sauntering across the stage, Evans accidentally lifted her dress too high and flashed the crowd as her nether regions were displayed on the big screen behind her.

“Wonder if Faith Evans was low key flashing us intentionally or just into the performance,” one concertgoer tweeted alongside the video.

It’s unclear whether Evans was wearing flesh-colored underwear or if she completely exposed her, ahem, womanhood, but that didn’t stop fans from going crazy over the clip.

“Aye, look. Let me see why Faith Evans is trending? Aw… No, Lord. Why..?” one user captioned a GIF of Tyler Perry‘s beloved character Madea rolling around on the floor.
