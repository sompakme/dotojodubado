Born as Richard Sean Salisbury on March 9, 1963, Sean Salisbury is an American actor and former CFL and NFL quarterback with an estimated net worth of $150 thousand.

A highly recruited quarterback who attended the Orange Glen High School that is based in Escondido, California, Salisbury chose to attend the University of Southern California over other famous universities like Arizona, Noter Dame, Arizona State, Stanford, BYU and University of California Los Angeles for college.

During his professional football career, Sean Salisbury became a part of CFL's Winnipeg Blue Bombers,  as well as Seattle Seahawks, San Diego Chargers, Minnesota Vikings, Houston Oilers and Indianapolis Colts. After his 10-year professional football career, Sean Salisbury was hired by ESPN as a NFL Analyst. Later on, he appeared on NFL Live and SportsCenter.
