Mom Details Nightmare Situation

In her video, TikToker Mrsbelleblake recalled how the incident left her fuming. Another mom at her daughter's dance class told her how Stephanie had claimed to have given birth to Delilah. My birth story that I went through."

Later in her video, Mrsbelleblake also comments that Stephanie "wasn't even in the picture when Delilah was born. Stephanie was in high school when Delilah was born." That's not all. Mom Resolves Her Crazy Situation With Her Daughter's Stepmom
