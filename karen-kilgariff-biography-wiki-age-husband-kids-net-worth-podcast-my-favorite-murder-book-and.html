Karen Kilgariff Stand Up

Karen began her career as a stand-up comedian in Sacramento in the 1990s. She has had several comedy roles on televisions including being a cast member on seasons 3 and 4 of Mr. Show, and W/ Bob & David.

She served as the head writer for The Rosie Show which was aired on the Oprah Winfrey Network and later served as the head writer for The Pete Holmes Show, which aired on TBS. In 2014, She recorded a musical comedy album Live at the Bootleg. She also produced Making History on FOX Broadcasting Company.

Karen Kilgariff Ellen

In 2003, Kilgariff was hired by Ellen DeGeneres to help write for her comedy special, Ellen DeGeneres: Here and Now, and they toured together as her opening act. Karen Kilgariff was then named head writer for the day time talk show The Ellen DeGeneres Show which was launched in September 2003.
