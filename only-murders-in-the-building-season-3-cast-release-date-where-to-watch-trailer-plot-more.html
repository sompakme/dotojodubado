Only Murders in the Building Season 3 – The hit Hulu comedy series Only Murders in the Building is back with a new season and a new mystery to solve. The show stars Martin, Martin Short, and Selena Gomez as the amateur sleuths who get involved in hilarious and dangerous situations as they try to crack the case.

Season 3 of Only Murders in the Building premiered on August 8, 2023, with two episodes, and will release new episodes every Tuesday on Hulu. The season will consist of 10 episodes, each about 30 minutes long. The season will also be available on Disney+ Hotstar in India.

Plot

Only Murders in the Building Season 3 follows Charles, Oliver, and Mabel as they try to solve the murder of Ben Glenroy, who died on stage during the opening night of Oliver’s Broadway play Death Rattle. The trio suspects that someone poisoned Ben with cyanide, but they have no idea who or why.

They decide to start a new podcast called Only Murders on Broadway to document their investigation and share their findings with their listeners. They also hope to save Oliver’s play from being canceled due to Ben’s death.

As they dig deeper into the case, they discover that Ben had many enemies and secrets and that his death may be connected to a larger conspiracy. They also encounter new challenges and dangers, such as a mysterious stalker, a rival podcast, and a vengeful killer.

Along the way, they also deal with their personal issues, such as Charles’ relationship with Mabel, Oliver’s career crisis, and Mabel’s past trauma. They also meet new friends and foes, such as Loretta, Marcus, Kimber, and Cinda.

The season will explore themes such as friendship, love, betrayal, ambition, and justice. It will also feature many twists and turns, as well as references to Broadway shows and theater culture.
