Directed by Steven Spielberg and based on a novel by Michael Crichton, Jurassic Park was first released in 1993 and has grown into a mega-blockbuster franchise. Though the original films took a nearly 15-year break in the early 2000s, Jurassic World brought the movies back to the big screen in 2015. Starring Chris Pratt and Bryce Dallas Howard, Jurassic World: Dominion, is currently in production and is bringing back original cast members such as Jeff Goldblum, Sam Neill, and Laura Dern.

10 Chris Pratt - Age 41, Height 6'2, Married

Christopher Michael Pratt is 41 years old. He was born on June 21st of 1979 and is 6’2. Previously married to Anna Farris in 2009, the couple divorced in 2018. They share a son named Jack and co-parent amicably. Pratt got remarried in 2019 to Katherine Schwarzenegger, and they welcomed a daughter this past August. Her name is Lyla Maria.

Bryce Dallas Howard was born on March 2nd of 1981 and is 39 years old. She is 5 foot 7 inches tall. A talented actress, she has received a Screen Actors Guild award for her role in The Help. Daughter of the famous director, Ron Howard, she has followed in her father’s footsteps by directing an episode of The Mandalorian.
