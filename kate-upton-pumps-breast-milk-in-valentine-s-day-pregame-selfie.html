Kate Upton Pumps Breast Milk in Valentine's Day 'Pregame' Selfie

July 24, 2024
blog

No secrets here! Before Kate Upton and her husband, Justin Verlander, could get to their romantic Valentine’s Day plans, the model had some business to take care of first — pumping milk.

“Valentine’s Day dinner pregame,” the Other Woman actress, 26, captioned a Thursday, February 14, mirror selfie. “#JustKeepPumpin.”

In the pic, Upton pulled up her top and held a breast pump in place. The Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model was dressed for date night in a pink skirt.

That wasn’t the only glimpse that the new mom gave of her V-Day. She also shared a selfie with her husband, 35. “My forever Valentine,” she wrote.

Upton and the Houston Astros pitcher tied the knot in 2017. The couple welcomed their first child together, a baby girl named Genevieve, one year later.

The professional baseball player and Upton haven’t made any plans to expand their brood just yet, though.

“I can’t even think about having more kids now,” she told Extra in January. “I’m just sleeping through the night now, so it’s still fresh in my mind. [Genevieve] actually is a really good baby. I feel like she’s a really cute mixture of us both.”
