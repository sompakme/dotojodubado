Kevin Hart: 25 Things You Dont Know About Me

August 20, 2024
blog

Kevin Hart may be the king of comedy these days, but during one of his first shows ever, he completely ripped his pants — and didn’t even know it! Read on to find out more facts about the 37-year-old actor in this exclusive list of 25 things you don’t know about him. And catch Hart in his new concert film, Kevin Hart: What Now?, in theaters now! 

1. My favorite childhood memory was when I was tall enough to get on roller coasters.

2. My dream costar would be Denzel Washington. If I ever do decide to go into a serious role, you need somebody to help bring that strong validation, and who better than him?

3. I idolize my mother [Nancy Hart], rest in peace. The best advice my mom gave me was that we don’t do things not to finish them.

4. In my downtime, I’m a hell of a poker player. I have poker night sometimes, but I work too much to stay true to it.

5. My best quality is my personality. I have the ability to make anybody feel comfortable. The energy that I put out, I get back.

6. I could watch Tropic Thunder over and over. It’s the funniest movie ever.

7. A guy got a tattoo of my face on his lower back. That was weird. I was like, “I don’t know what you did that for, sir. I don’t know why it’s there. I don’t really know what I’m supposed to do after seeing that. I guess thank you.” 

8. My favorite book is Of Mice and Men, by John Steinbeck. I read it a long time ago. I like that and a book called Monsters, by Walter Dean Myers.

9. If I could meet anyone, I would choose Robin Williams. I would simply say, “You were loved. Your stamp on comedy is something that definitely wasn’t overlooked.”

10. When I first started performing in theaters, I was performing in Washington, D.C. I had on some cool nylon sweatpants. I was telling a joke, jumped in the air, landed and ripped my pants in the front. But I didn’t know. So the girl in the front row wrote a note and slid it on stage. It said something like, “Kevin, your whole area is out right now.”

11. My biggest phobia is reptiles. Dogs are fine; reptiles get up under my skin. Snakes, lizards, things of that nature. I can’t do it.

12. My worst physical qualities are my two big toenails. They look like burned sunflower seeds.

13. My kids [Heaven, 11, and Hendrix, 8] definitely think I tell dad jokes. You don’t realize you tell dad jokes until your kids go, “Dad, stop telling dad jokes.”

14. My favorite thing about being a dad of my kids is doing things that you didn’t do as a kid with your kids. We have Taco Tuesday Night.

15. In 2015, 53,000 people came to see me perform at a football stadium in Philadelphia. That was one of the most amazing days of my life. I felt like I had to do that day its service, so I turned the stand-up concert into this movie [Kevin Hart: What Now?].

16. My biggest regret is not giving the full 110 percent that I should have given in school.

17. I can play the spoons. I just grab spoons and clink them together.

18. It’s very hard to have a celebrity crush because my wife [model Eniko Parrish, 32] is so amazing.

19. The best thing about my wife is her ability to make me better.

20. I always wish that I had starred in Goodfellas. Joe Pesci’s character, Tommy, is a dream role.

21. If I weren’t an actor, I’d probably be a hand model.

22. My favorite sport to watch and play is basketball. My favorite team is the Philadelphia 76ers, but don’t tell anybody I said that.

23. I love black jeans and a nice T-shirt. I have maybe 30 pairs of black jeans.

24. My go-to joke is, “If I was taller, would I have a chance?” (I’m 5-foot-5.)

25. I listen to “Sorry,” by Justin Bieber, on repeat. I am 100 percent a Belieber. 

Thank You!
