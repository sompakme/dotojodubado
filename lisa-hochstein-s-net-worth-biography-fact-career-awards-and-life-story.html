Lisa Hochstein net worth: Lisa Hochstein is a former Playboy model and reality TV star who has a net worth of $30 million. Lisa is most famous for being one of the cast members of The Real Housewives of Miami. Lisa Hochstein earned her net worth by marrying prominent Miami plastic surgeon Dr. Leonard “Lenny” Hochstein. Born Lisa Marie Mackay, she has made three appearances in Playboy magazine, once in Playboy’s Sexy Girls Next Door, once on the cover of Playboy’s Vixens and once again in Playboy’s Voluptuous Vixens. Interestingly, Lisa Hochstein married a plastic surgeon two years AFTER appearing in Playboy, usually it’s the other way around right? But perhaps that’s how they met? Lisa married Leonard Hochstein on October 24th, 2009 at their palatial Miami mansion. The couple has since traveled the world together. Living it up in exotic locales such as St. Tropez, Paris and Venice Italy. But they still make Miami their home base.

Lisa joined model Joanna Krupa as the two newest cast members of Bravo’s The Real Housewives of Miami which began filming in February 2012. Lisa and Joanna was accompanied by returning housewives Marysol Patton, Audriana de Moura and Lea Black. Alexia Echevarria is no longer a regular cast member but will be making guest appearances. NBA star Scottie Pippen’s wife Larsa will not be returning. Neither will NBA star Glen Rice’s ex-wife Cristy.

Lisa’s husband Dr. Lenny has actually appeared on Bravo before. Back in 2004, at the height of the popularity of FX Networks “Nip/Tuck”, Dr. Hochstein starred in the Bravo reality show “Miami Slice”.
