What Is FairyTail Pet Care?

Looking for a way to include your beloved furry friend in your special day? Look no further than FairyTail Pet Care, a franchise-based pet wedding service that specializes in making your wedding day memorable for both you and your pet.

At FairyTail Pet Care, we understand that pets are an important part of your family, and many couples want to include them in their wedding celebrations. That’s why we offer a range of services to ensure that your pet is well taken care of and can be by your side on your big day.

Our base wedding service package starts at $600, which includes 3 hours of pet care and transportation. We provide professional pet care providers who are experienced in handling pets and making sure they feel comfortable and loved throughout the event.

In addition to pet care, we also offer coordination with vendors to ensure that everything runs smoothly on your wedding day. Whether it’s arranging floral collars or tuxedo bandanas for your pets or coordinating their arrival and departure, we take care of all the details so you can focus on enjoying your special day.

Here are some key features of our FairyTail Pet Care wedding service:

  • Professional pet care providers
  • Transportation for your pets
  • Coordination with vendors
  • Accessories such as tuxedo bandanas or floral collars

Additionally, we offer a range of customizable and add-on services to cater to your specific needs. From pet grooming to pet training sessions, we are dedicated to ensuring that your pet is at their best on your wedding day.

If you’re interested in joining the FairyTail Pet Care family, we also offer franchise opportunities. The cost to open a FairyTail Pet Care franchise ranges from $12,500 to $37,800, depending on the location and level of support required.

Who Is The Founder Of FairyTail Pet Care?

FairyTail Pet Care was founded by Ilana Mobely-Karcinski and Kelly Nova in 2015. They are childhood friends who had the idea for the company while working at a hotel in Tampa. Both founders have a deep love for animals and wanted to provide a service that allowed pets to be included in weddings.

How Was The Shark Tank Pitch Of FairyTail Pet Care?

During their FairyTail Pet Care Shark Tank pitch, Ilana and Kelly presented their investment request and business model to the sharks. They were seeking a $75,000 investment in exchange for 10% equity in the company, which provides pet care services for weddings. FairyTail Pet Care offers 3 hours of pet care and transportation for a base price of $600, with an additional $100 for extra hours.

FairyTail Pet Care pays a 9% royalty on gross sales to franchise owners. In 2022, the company generated sales of $123,000 with an impressive net profit margin of 70%. Their strong sales and profit margin made them an attractive investment opportunity for the sharks.

Barbara Corcoran, renowned for her business acumen, recognized the potential of FairyTail Pet Care and made a counteroffer. She offered $75,000 for 25% equity in the company. After negotiation, Barbara and the founders agreed on a final deal: $75,000 for 22% equity.
