Who is Diane Downs?

Full name Elizabeth Diane Frederickson Downs, she is a convicted murderer of American descent. She was charged and then later convicted of shooting her three children. Even though she intended to kill all three, one child died as a result. 

She would tell the police that she was the victim of a carjack. In order to prove her claim, she would also shoot herself to convince the authorities. 

Her conviction was carried out in 1984, and she would then be sentenced to prison for life. 

In 1987, she would briefly escape her capture but would then be recaptured by the authorities. She has been denied parole three times: one in December of 2008, December of 2010 and then in 2021.

Her story has been adopted in a book by Ann Rule. She has also been a topic of a movie called Small Sacrifices.
