How Affleck Is Helping Garner Grieve

"He has been checking in on her and making sure she knows he is there for her since her dad's passing. It was the kids' grandfather and Ben and him always got along,” the insider explained. “He considered him family and always will."

Despite the tragedy, the source said Garner is "beyond dealing with this loss," and she’s " in a very happy place and gets along."

They also made reference to Lopez, saying she and Garner have "gotten closer, which is nice for both of them."

Even though divorce rumors have been following Jen and Ben for months, the source claims things are going good for the couple. “They are doing well and are very happy together,” they dished. “Their kids bring them even closer together and keep them humble. Jen and Ben are supportive of one another and just want the best for their relationship and family.”

The insider added, “Their kids love being a blended family and mesh well together. They're all kind and get along well."

How Jennifer Garner’s Dad Died

Garner originally announced her father’s death in a touching tribute made to Instagram. “My dad passed peacefully Saturday afternoon. We were with him, singing Amazing Grace as he left us (did we carry him across or scare him away— valid question,)” she wrote.

“While there is no tragedy in the death of an 85-year-old man who lived a healthy, wonderful life, I know grief is unavoidable, waiting around unexpected corners,” she went on.

"There is so much to say about my dad— my sisters and I will never be done talking about how wonderful he was, so bear with us— but for today I share these memories with my appreciation for the kind and brilliant man, father, and grandfather he was, as well as the loving legacy he left behind,” Garner continued.
