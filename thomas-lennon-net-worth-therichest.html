Thomas Lennon is a renowned American actor-director, producer, and screenwriter with an estimated net worth of $8 million. This comedian is known for his role as Lieutenant Jim Dangle in the comedy central series Reno 911 and a cast member on MTV’s The State.

He was born to Timothy Lennon and Kathleen in Oak Park in the year 1970, Illinois. He attended Oak Park and River Forest High school where he graduated in 1988. When he was sixteen, he attended a theater camp at Northwestern University where he met Kerri Kenney who would later become a co-worker. They joined The New Group which was later called The State. He has written impressive screenplays in conjunction with Ben Garant. He is married to actress Jenny Robertson and they have one son Oliver with whom they live with in Los Angeles.
