Richard Hamilton and T.J. Lottie

Meet T.J. Lottie Hamilton, wife of NBA star Richard Hamilton.

Richard met T.J. while on vacation in 2005 at the Casa Grande Suite Hotel in Miami. After asking where Lottie worked in L.A., he courted her with phone calls and flowers until the two started dating.

After three years together, the couple returned to Casa Grande to celebrate T.J’s birthday. Instead, Hamilton showed her a video that he’d made with their son. Richard says, “At the end of the video, I recorded Deuce shouting, ‘Mommy, please say yes!’”

The couple married on July 11, 2009 at the Boca Raton Resort & Club in front of guests including LeBron James and several of Hamilton’s Pistons teammates.
