Seth Rollins To Cody Rhodes: Do You Want To Be Like Hulk Hogan Or Dusty Rhodes?

August 29, 2024
blog

Seth Rollins sent yet another message to Cody Rhodes.

Over the past few days, World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins has been trying to convince Cody Rhodes to face him at WrestleMania 40. Rhodes won the Men’s Royal Rumble, seemingly setting the stage for a rematch with Undisputed WWE Universal Champion Roman Reigns. But on the January 29 episode of WWE RAW, Rollins told Rhodes he should face him instead.

Rollins has since posted videos on Twitter in which he has continued his attempt to persuade Rhodes. In his latest tweet, Rollins sent another message to Rhodes by asking him whether he wanted to be like Hulk Hogan or his father, Dusty Rhodes.

“Cody, ask yourself a question,” Seth Rollins said, “Do you want to be like the immortal Hulk Hogan, or do you want to be like ‘The American Dream’ Dusty Rhodes? It’s time for you to decide what kind of champion you want to be.”

Rhodes responded to Rollins by saying he would be at the February 2 episode of WWE SmackDown. WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on Friday.
