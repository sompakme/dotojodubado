Kaley Cuoco is a famous American actress with an estimated net worth of $55 million. Kaley Christine Cuoco was born on November 30, 1985, in Camarillo, California. Her mother is of English, and German ancestry and her father came from Italian descent. When Cuoco was a child, she was a regionally ranked amateur tennis player. It was a sport she took up when she was only three years old.

Kaley Cuoco started her career in acting when she appeared in the action thriller film, Virtuosity in 1995. Afterward, she acted in several films and TV series such as Growing Up Brady, Alley Cats Strikes, My So-Called Life, 7th Heaven, The Hollow, Charmed, and Prison Break. Her breakthrough role was Bridget Hennessy in the ABC sitcom 8 Simple Rules from 2002 to 2005. Later on, Cuoco appeared as Billie Jenkins in the television series Charmed's final series from 2005-2006.

Cuoco got her big break when she played the role of Penny on The Big Bang Theory since 2007. It is a CBS sitcom where she received the People's Choice, Critic's Choice, and Satellite Awards. She also appeared in films like Hop (2011), Authors Anonymous (2014), and To Be Fat Like Me (2007). She appeared on the cover of Allure magazine in 2011. Kaley was in sixth place in FHM's annual list of 100 Sexiest Women last 2013. In 2014, she received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
