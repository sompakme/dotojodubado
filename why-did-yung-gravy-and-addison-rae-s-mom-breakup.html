Why Did Yung Gravy And Addison Raes Mom Breakup?

Yung Gravy made headlines at the 2022 MTV Music Video Awards when he rocked the red carpet with Sheri Easterling, the mother of social media superstar Addison Rae, in August 2022. The pair appeared to be in full couple mode, as they packed on the PDA for the cameras. The red carpet date came a month after the pair were seen being flirtatious over social media.They were certainly an unexpected couple, and reports say that Addison was “embarrassed” her mom was seeing a much younger man. But the romance fizzled out, as it was reported by October 2022 the two had broken up.So, what went wrong between Yung Gravy and Sheri? Yung Gravy Was Just A Rebound For Sheri

Only a month before Yung Gravy and Sheri were talked the red carpet together, her husband (Addison’s father), Monty Lopez, was exposed for infidelity in July 2022. He was allegedly having affairs with women – around the same age as his social media famous daughter.

One of his mistresses, Renee Ash, spoke to Page Six about their affair. She says Monty told her his marriage to Sheri was over. But Renee broke up with him after she discovered he was seeing other women at the same time.

“Unfortunately, he misled me on his marriage, he lied to me,” Renee said. “I loved him, and I believed him. When I found out about the other young girls, my heart broke.”

Sheri subsequently released a statement, implying her marriage to Monty was over.

“Personal matters being brought public are always challenging and overwhelming for anyone involved,” she said. “I will be okay. My biggest concern is -and always will be- my children and their fragile hearts and minds. I will always do my best to protect them.”

Monty and Sheri went on to finalize their divorce by November 2022.

But Sheri was linked to Yung Gravy mere weeks after Monty’s cheating was exposed. The timing seems to suggest she was using him as a rebound and/or a way to distract the public’s attention.

Yung Gravy Might've Wanted A Career Boost, According To Rumors

Given that Yung Gravy seemed like a rebound for Sheri, it’s not surprising the romance didn’t last. She’s likely still reeling from the divorce and possible not looking to settle down.

For Yung Gravy, the rapper also doesn’t seem interested in a serious commitment. He was likely interested in romancing Sheri because she fits his brand – he’s admitted to liking older women. Many of his songs mention having a preference for “MILFs.” For example, in “Mr. Clean,” he raps, “I’m with your mom in the kitchen, makin’ blueberry muffins.”

Yung Gravy is also just up-and-coming in the music publicity. They say any publicity is good publicity. In the wake of her divorce, being the first guy romantically linked to Sheri is instantly headline-making (given that she’s the mom to Addison Rae, who has 39.5 million Instagram followers and 99.7 million TikTok followers.

Yung Gravy may have seen stepping out with Sheri as a way to boost his career and gain a bit of notoriety.

Yung Gravy And Sheri’s Relationship May Have Been A PR Stunt

Sheri wanted to distract from her embarrassing divorce, while Yung Gravy wanted to boost his career. But that’s not the only evidence the relationship was never meant to be long-term. Sources swear it was all one big PR stunt.

In August, a source told Page Six the two had faked their red carpet appearance, saying, “It’s a PR relationship. It’s kissing, but it’s not real.” The insider added that Sheri isn’t the rapper’s type. In fact, his references to liking older women are just part of an act and he prefers women closer in age to himself.

However, Yung Gravy swears he’s still on good terms with Sheri and that they even communicate.

“We still keep in touch a lot,” he said in late October. 2022 “It wouldn’t make sense for us to date because we live in different cities, but we keep in touch. Any show I have in Louisiana, she’ll be there.”

He also said that he didn’t expect people to assume he and Sheri were dating when he took her to the VMAs in August.

“When I brought [Easterling], everyone was instantly like, ‘Oh, they’re dating.’ Tons of rumours started about her and I being pregnant and all this stuff,” he explained. “We had been flirting online, I wanted a cute first date and I thought, ‘Let’s do this.’ I guess that’s not how it works with these fame type people.”

Whether it was a PR stunt or a short-lived flirtation, there’s no evidence to suggest Yung Gravy and Sheri were ever in it for the long haul.
