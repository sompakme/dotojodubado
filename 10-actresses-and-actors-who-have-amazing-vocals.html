10 Actresses and Actors Who Have Amazing Vocals

September 05, 2024
blog

Most Hollywood actors and actresses have proven that they have many hidden skills that their fans don't always know about. Singing is among the many hidden talents that these celebrities have that might leave their fans in shock once they decide to reveal their vocal ability with the world.

Having amazing vocals while also being a great actor or actress gives certain celebs the upper hand when it comes to choosing roles that will require singing. Such abilities have led these particular celebrities towards being labled double threats as they can both sing and act. Below are 10 of the best actress and actors whose vocal skills will leave their listeners asking for more.

10 Seth MacFarlane

He is a man who wears many hats, and fortunately, he wears them successfully. Seth MacFarlane has one of the most diverse voices in Hollywood as seen in hilarious animated shows like Family Guy and American Dad.

Seth voices several characters in the show earning respect from his peers for his outstanding voice acting. Seth is a good actor and also an excellent singer to the extent of having his songs getting four Grammy nominations.

9 Jared Leto

He is one of the most talented and gorgeous men in Hollywood. Jared Leto has continued to show success in his acting and singing careers earning himself an Oscar award and several music awards from his rock band Thirty Seconds to Mars.

His singing ability earned him the position of lead singer in his group, proving just how talented he is. The band has successfully sold millions of copies of their album and their songs have charted high on the billboards plenty of times.

8 Bradley Cooper

Bradley Cooper's vocal abilities did not shock many fans due to his naturally deep and soothing voice. Lady Gaga, his co-star in the movie A Star Is Born, admitted how Bradley's voice caught her off gourd as it was so unexpected.

Bradley's collaboration with Lady Gaga on the song "Shallow" gained a lot of popularity. It earned several awards, including two Grammy awards for the best-written song for visual media and the best pop collaboration.

7 Wayne Brady

If anyone had doubts that Wayne Brady can carry a tune, they must have been proven wrong when Wayne won The Masked Singer season 2 crown. His singing ability has been praised by many.

6 Hugh Jackman

Hugh Jackman's acting ability is unmatched as is he is one of the highest-paid actors in Hollywood. He has proven himself to be one of the best actors who can play roles in musical films.

His singing ability has earned him glowing reviews from both professional singers and voice coaches. His recent role in the movie, The Greatest Showman, left fans across the world astonished by his vocal abilities and range and how effortlessly he played the part.

5 Emma Stone

Many fans of the movie La La Land can attest how good a singer Emma Stone is. She shocked many of her followers when she played Mia's role in the musical La La Land, as many were unaware, she could sing so well.

While she is always shy displaying her vocal abilities, her singing clips on the internet and previous acting in a musical prove that she can carry a tune so well.
