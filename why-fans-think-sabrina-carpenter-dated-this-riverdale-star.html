He said in an interview, “I always wanted to be good at sports but I just wasn’t, my dad always says I’m a late bloomer.” During his junior high school years, he had participated in his first school play and then began performing in local theaters in Ohio. He even got a role in theatre productions such as Bloody Bloody AndrewJackson and Romeo & Juliet.
