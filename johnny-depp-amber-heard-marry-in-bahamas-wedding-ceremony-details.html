Johnny Depp, Amber Heard Marry in Bahamas: Wedding Ceremony Details

July 26, 2024
blog

Depp is wed — again! Days after Johnny Depp and Amber Heard tied the knot at home, the couple wed (yet again!) in a beautiful beach ceremony in the Bahamas on Sunday, Feb. 8, sources confirm to Us Weekly.

The ceremony took place in a small cove on a white sand beach, with a few dozen chairs set up on both sides of an aisle that trailed down to the altar which included the ocean as a backdrop.

According to one insider, actor/director Paul Bettany was there to witness the stars exchange vows. Depp's two children, daughter Lily-Rose, 15, and son Jack, 12, whom he shares with ex Vanessa Paradis, were there to see their dad get hitched.

For most of the weekend, guests hung out on the beach, drank, played music, went snorkeling, and enjoyed the private affair.

The source tells Us that the wedding was on the "smaller side" and more intimate than the couple's star-studded engagement party last March, which was attended by Mandy Moore, Steven Tyler, and Marilyn Manson.

Us confirmed the news last week that the Rum Diary costars first married on Feb. 3, at Depp's West Hollywood home. "Things are in a great place with them," a source told Us, adding that their tropical wedding would be "really simple" too.

Depp, 51, and Heard, 28, first met on the 2009 set of The Rum Diary, and stepped out officially as a couple in 2011. Before Heard, Depp and French actress Paradis were together for a whopping 14 years before they split in June 2012. Though he never wed Paradis in their long-term relationship, Depp was once married for two years back in the mid-'80s to Lori Anne Allison.
