Noma Dumezweni’s Family: A Look at Her Parents, Nomanono Isaacs & Thami Dumezweni

A Distinguished Career

Dumezweni’s presence graced the original Broadway run of the same production, where she garnered a nomination for the prestigious Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in a Play in 2018.

Born on July 28, 1969, Noma Dumezweni’s journey began in Mbabane, Swaziland, now known as Eswatini. Her family roots are deeply embedded in South Africa, where her parents, Nomanono Isaacs and Thami Dumezweni, originate. This connection to South Africa has undoubtedly influenced Dumezweni’s identity and artistic sensibilities.

Dumezweni’s life story is one of resilience, adaptability, and growth. Her family’s journey led them across various countries, including Botswana, Kenya, and Uganda, before they found refuge in England. They arrived in England in 1992 as refugees, seeking a better life and opportunities.

Honoring Nomanono Isaacs and Thami Dumezweni
