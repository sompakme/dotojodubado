Travis Explained Why His Past Posts Embarrass Him

In response, Travis’ brother Jason said, “I think we all have tweets that we would love to have deleted.”

“I was just using Twitter as, like, a diary,” Travis explained of why he finds his past posts embarrassing. “I’m just out here saying nonsense.”
