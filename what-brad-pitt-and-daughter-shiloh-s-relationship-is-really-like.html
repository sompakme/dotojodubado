Shiloh Jolie Pitt Had A Small Role In 'The Curious Case Of Benjamin Button'

Shiloh Nouvel Jolie-Pitt is Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt's first biological daughter. To avoid the massive press scrutiny, the then couple traveled to Namibia for Jolie to give birth. The aim was for the money to go to charity, rather than allowing paparazzi to take the photographs. Shiloh and her father grew a strong bond, so much so that when she was two, Pitt took his daughter on set with him.
