A former National Football League cornerback, Douglas Terrell Buckley (born June 7, 1971) is an American football cornerback who played for the New York Giants. While at the University of Alabama, Buckley competed in professional baseball with the Mobile BaySharks of the Texas-Louisiana League. He has the distinction of being the only player with more than 50 interceptions who has never played in a pro bowl. Buckley established an NFL record by returning a punt for a touchdown against Cincinnati during his rookie season in 1992, setting a mark that has stood since.
