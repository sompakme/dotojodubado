This Is The All-Star Cast Of Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery

September 07, 2024
blog

Daniel Craig is back as Benoit Blanc, investigating a whole new group of suspects in the sequel to the 2019 hit Knives Out.

This new film, Glass Onion, sees Craig's detective attending a murder mystery party at a lavish private estate on a Greek island, hosted by a tech billionaire for his rich friends. The weekend goes sideways when a real-life murder takes place.

The film is one of two Knives Out sequels to have been ordered by Netflix but will have a limited cinema release before it appears on the streamer.

Like the original murder mystery, the cast of Glass Onion, is stacked with Hollywood heavyweights. Benoit will investigate all these people, some of whom may not end the movie alive, and learn their backstory and possible motive.

So who is in the cast of Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery?

8 Daniel Craig Plays Benoit Blanc

Daniel Craig plays Benoit Blanc, the eccentric world-famous detective, who previously helped to crack the case of Harlan Thrombey's death in Knives Out. The 54-year-old is the only cast member to return from the 2019 movie.

Craig is best known for playing James Bond in five films including this year's No Time To Die. The English actor has also starred in films such as The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, The Golden Compass, Layer Cake and Logan Lucky. The BAFTA-winning actor has been married to Rachel Weisz since 2011.

7 Edward Norton Plays Miles Bron

Edward Noton stars as Miles Bron, a tech billionaire who owns the private Greek island the majority of the film takes place on. His character has been compared to Elon Musk, due to his obsession with 'disrupting' industries. The actor is known for a range of roles across genres.

Norton gained critical acclaim for his debut in Primal Fear, which earned him a Golden Globe for Best Supporting Actor and an Academy Award nomination. His role in American History X earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor and Birdman a Supporting Actor nomination. He also starred in Fight Club, Red Dragon and The Grand Budapest Hotel.

6 Kate Hudson Plays Birdie Jay

Kate Hudson stars as Birdie, a former model and fashion editor who was canceled for making a number of blunders online and making comments deemed racist. She is an air-headed character who appears to not get the joke until minutes later.

Forty-three-year-old Hudson is the daughter of Goldie Hawn. She rose to international fame with her portrayal of Penny Lane in Almost Famous, for which she won a Golden Globe for Best Supporting Actress and received an Oscar nomination. She is known for making romantic comedies like Alex & Emma, How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days, My Best Friend's Girl and Bride Wars.

5 Jessica Henwick Plays Peg

Jessica Henwick's Peg is Birdie's protective assistant who has to hide her phone to stop her from getting into scandals. She based the role on her own experience as a production assistant.

British born Henwick is known for appearing in the Netflix show Iron Fist, as well as playing Nymeria Sands in Game of Thrones. She plays X-wing pilot Jessika Pava in Star Wars: The Force Awakens and Bugs in 2021's The Matrix Resurrections.

4 Janelle Monáe Plays Cassandra 'Andi' Brand

Janelle Monae stars as Cassandra, also known as Andi, is a tech entrepreneur and is Miles' bitter ex-business partner. Much of the plot centers on the bust-up Andi and Miles had, which led to a high profile court case.

Thirty-six-year-old Janelle Monae is a prolific singer and rapper who has earned eight Grammy nominations over her career. In 2016, Monáe made her film debut in Hidden Figures as NASA mathematician and aerospace engineer Mary Jackson, and also starred in Moonlight.

3 Kathryn Hahn Plays Claire Debella

Kathryn Hahn plays Claire, a Connecticut governor who is running for a position in the Senate. She is a married mom who appears to have liberal values.

Hahn was recently seen in the Disney + series WandaVision, and will be getting her own spin-off series Agatha: Coven of Chaos. She started her career in supporting roles in the likes How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days, Step Brothers, We're the Millers, and The Secret Life of Walter Mitty. She also played the grief counselor Lily Lebowski in the NBC crime drama series Crossing Jordan, guest starred in Parks and Recreation and starred in Transparent.

2 Leslie Odom Jr. Plays Lionel Toussaint

Lionel is a scientist who works for Miles but is concerned about the billionaire's plans. Forty-one-year-old Leslie Odom Jr is best known for playing Aaron Burr in the original run of Hamilton, which earned him a 2016 Tony Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role in a Musical and a Grammy Award for Best Musical Theater Album.

He has also starred in films including The Many Saints of Newark and Murder on the Orient Express. He gained acclaim and numerous nominations for his role as Sam Cooke in One Night In Miami. He has also released four albums and an inspirational book.

1 Madelyn Cline And Dave Bautista Play Whiskey And Duke

Bautista's Duke is a controversial YouTube star known for his support of the NRA and the men's rights movement, Cline plays his girlfriend Whiskey.

Ex-wrestler Bautista is best known for his role as Drax in The Guardians of the Galaxy films. He has also appeared in See, Dune, Spectre and Blade Runner 2049. Madelyn Cline is know for playing Sarah Cameron on the Netflix drama series Outer Banks. She also had small recurring roles in The Originals and Stranger Things.
