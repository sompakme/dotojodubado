There once was a time that tattoos were only displayed on the world's most undesirable people, including criminals, gang members, and those with a connection to the underworld. Through the evolution of mainstream urban culture, ink art became popularized by prominent figures in the entertainment industry. This soon evolved to embrace the young, young at heart, and professionals from all walks of life, that contrary to their tattooed predecessors, flocked towards tattoo art as a form of expression rather than an act of retaliation. The Things reports on how the rich and famous have turned tattoos into not only a mainstream art form but also have crafted the opportunity for the most talented tattoo artists in the world to truly make a name for themselves in the industry. When a celebrity is ready to have their bodies permanently inked, the world takes note on who the talented artist is that is entrusted with this honor, and they're definitely paid accordingly. Let's take a look at the richest celebrity tattoo artists that have a waitlist that's just as long as their price tag is.

Nikko Hurtado: $250,000

Nikko Hurtado is the man with a plan when it comes full colored portrait tattoo art. His net worth is reported to be within the range of roughly $250,000 and continues to grow in leaps and bounds. Charging just over $150 per hour, Nikko has been trusted to tattoo some pretty big names, including Amber Rose, Tess Holliday, and Cheryl Cole. His celebrity clients rave about the outcome after seeing Hurtado, and his name is quickly catching on.

Scott Campbell: $1 Million

Thanks to his incredible talents in the realm of celebrity tattoos, Scott Campbell has earned a cool $1 million fortune and continues to demonstrate traction upwards. He has done tattoos for the likes of Robert Downey Jr., Orlando Bloom, Heath Ledger, Helena Christensen, rocker Courtney Love, and the truly legendary designer Marc Jacobs. The Talko reports that Scott charges $2,000 per hour to start a job, and each subsequent hour is billed out at roughly $200.

Dr. Woo: $1.5 Million

Dr. Woo has had the honor of doing tattoos on some of the biggest names in entertainment, including Miley Cyrus, Hailey Bieber, Ellie Goulding, Drake, and Zoe Kravitz. Specializing in realism art, he has also inked Demi Lovato and has been credited for his precision and attention to detail. He's specially recognized for the intricately designed elephant tattoo he did for Cara Delevingne, which she proudly displays on her wrist for the world to see.

Jon Boy: $1.5 Million

Jonathan Valena, known by the stars by the name JonBoy, has the privilege of tattooing Hailey Baldwin and Kendall Jenner and has been widely recognized for his simple, ittie-bittie tattoo designs. His specialty is carefully crafted, perfectly placed small tattoos that send simple, powerful statements.

Jonathan Shaw: $3 Million

Jonathan Shaw has made a jaw-dropping $3 million by tattooing some of the biggest names in entertainment, including Iggy Pop, Johnny Dep-, The Ramones, and the legendary Tupac Shakur. He also lays claims to having tattooed Marilyn Manson, Orlando Bloom, and Kate Moss. His client list has boosted his reputation, and there's a hefty waitlist to get his attention for a new tattoo design.

Bob Tyrrell: $3 Million

Bob Tyrrell has been a prominently recognized tattoo artist for the rich and famous for several years, and his work is in very high demand. He rose to fame after tattooing Kid Rock's back and charges in excess of $155 per hour for his talents. He's highly competitive and has appeared on tattoo competition shows LA Ink and London Ink. Today, Tyrrell boasts a $3 million net worth valuation.

Bang Bang: $5 Million

Keith "Bang Bang" McCurdy has a client list that is as impressive as his talent is. He has been trusted to tattoo Rihanna and has been her go-to for a number of tattoos over the years. He has also inked Katy Perry, Ruby Rose, Justin Bieber, and Demi Lovato, making him one of the biggest names in the industry, and affording him the opportunity to charge accordingly for his services, taking him right to a $5 million net worth.

Mark Mahoney: $6 Million

Mark Mahoney is one of the few people that can say he has tattooed the likes of The Notorious B.I.G. and Tupac Shakur. His edgy style and incredible reputation have also driven stars like Angelina Jolie, Johnny Depp, Rihanna, Jared Leto, David Beckham, and Adele to tap into his talents and get their tattoos done by him as well. His $6 million net worth was achieved after demanding $500 - $1000 per hour for his work.

Paul Booth: $7 Million

Paul Booth is known as the rock band tattoo artist and has established quite a name for himself after inking Slipknot, Pantera, Sepultura, and Mudvayne. With a client list as prominent as this one, Booth has been able to demand $300 per hour, and his cutting-edge designs have led him to his $7 million net worth valuation.

Kat Von D: $20 Million

Kat Von D is arguably the biggest name on the celebrity tattoo scene, and her portfolio boasts names such as Beyoncé, Miley Cyrus, Demi Lovato, Margaret Cho, and Eve. She is said to have a $20 million net worth and charges roughly $200 an hour for basic tattoo designs. Kat Von D launched to fame after being recognized for being trusted to work on the tattoos of such high-profile clients. She seized the moment and used her moment in the spotlight to create her own make-up line, which has been wildly successful. Her current net worth sits at an astounding $20 million.
